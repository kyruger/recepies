Mantı

for the dough;

  • 3.5 cups flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 egg

for inside;

  • 250 g of low-fat minced meat
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • Half a teaspoon of black pepper
  • Half a teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

for sauce

  • Garlic Yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon of mint
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • Half a teaspoon of sumac
  1. Let's add 3.5 cups of flour and 1 teaspoon of salt to a deep mixing bowl then make a hole in the midle of the flour by stirring

  2. add 1 egg to the middle and continue mixing.

  3. While stirring, let's gradually add 1 cup of warm water.

  4. knead the dough until it reaches a consistency that will be neither too hard nor soft.

  5. cover the ready-made dough with cling film and let it rest.

  6. While the dough is resting, let's add 250 grams of low-fat minced meat to a suitable mixing bowl.

  7. Then add 1 medium onion, half a teaspoon of black pepper, 1 teaspoon of salt, half a teaspoon of chili powder and mix well.

  8. Let's take the dough to the counter we sprinkled with flour, let's collect the dough by kneading. Let's divide the dough into 3 equal parts.

  9. turn the dough we have separated into meringues. Open the meringues we prepared on the counter we sprinkled with flour with a rolling pin so that they are thicker than the dough one by one.

  10. Let's cut the dough we have opened into small squares with a knife. Let's put it in small pieces in the minced meat mixture we have prepared in the middle of the squares we have cut.

  11. Give the ravioli shape by combining the 4 corners of the dough in the middle. Collect the ravioli that we shaped into a tray that we floured the bottom of and let's freeze the tray by taking it to the freezer.

  12. You can store the dumplings we took from the freezer in koroplast double ziplock bags and store them in the freezer to cook later. Let's boil water in a suitable saucepan.

  13. Let's cook the dumplings we took from the freezer for 15-20 minutes by adding them to the water. Let's add 2 tablespoons of butter to a suitable sauce pan, then add 2 tablespoons of tomato paste and mix well.

  14. Let's add the tomato paste sauce we have prepared to the cooked ravioli and continue cooking by stirring. Let's take the cooked ravioli on a deep plate and pour garlic or regular yogurt over it. Our ravioli recipe is ready. You can serve it by adding pepper flakes, mint and sumac.
